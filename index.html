Danish Delights is a family owned and operated bakery on Hillside Road in Dunedin.

Products

We sell a wide range of Danish and classic bakery treats. We keep an update to date list of products and prices on Google Drive which you can find here.

Placing an order

If you'd like to place an order give us a call at 03 455 4808, an email at orders.danish@gmail.com, or a text at 022 37 57 643. We are happy to reserve a few things for you on the day, but larger orders must be placed before close of business the day before. We keep a Google sheet if you'd like to know our prices and products before ordering.
