Danish Delights is a family owned and operated bakery on Hillside Road in Dunedin.

Products

We sell a wide range of Danish and classic bakery treats. We keep an update to date list of products and prices on Google Drive which you can find here.

Placing an order

If you'd like to place an order, give us a call at 03 455 4808. Orders can be handled as long as they are placed before 3pm the day before. You are also welcome to call and see if we have what you are looking for in stock.
